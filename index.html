Quick Guide

  • Drawing
  • Left Click empty space: start wall
  • Left Click again: finish wall
  • Selection
  • Left Click wall: select
  • Ctrl + Left Click wall: multi-select
  • Left Click empty: clear selection
  • Editing
  • Left Drag selected: move walls
  • Right Click wall: delete
  • Right Click selected: delete all selected
  • Navigation
  • Mouse Wheel zoom in/out
  • Shift + Left Drag pan view
  • Middle Mouse Drag pan view
  • 3D Controls
  • Ctrl + Middle Drag orbit camera
  • Alt + Middle Drag rotate camera
