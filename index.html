Quick Guide

  • Left Click on empty space to start drawing a wall
  • Left Click again to finish the wall
  • Left Click on a wall to select it
  • Ctrl + Left Click to select multiple walls
  • Right Click on a wall to delete it
  • Right Click on a selected wall to delete all selected walls
  • Left Click + drag on selected walls to move them
  • Shift + Left Click + drag to pan the view
  • Middle Mouse + drag to pan the view
  • Mouse Wheel to zoom in/out
  • Left Click on empty space to clear selection
  • Use the toolbar buttons to switch between 2D/3D views
  • Use Zoom Extend to fit all walls in view
